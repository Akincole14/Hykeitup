Our Story

An Impulse Idea That
Became a Movement

Hyke It Up was an impulse idea our Founder Sable created in April 2024. After searching for local hiking clubs without success, she decided to create one with co-founder Akin.

A community for millennials to meet up once a month across different locations in the U.K to get fit and explore the outdoors while networking and meeting new people.

The community aims to help millennials make friends, build community vibes, and support one another through life's journey — on the trail and beyond.

  • Community-led, community-owned
  • All fitness levels welcome
  • Safety briefings on every hyke
  • No hiking experience needed to join
What We Stand For

Our Values

🧭

Adventure

We seek out routes that challenge and inspire — from misty moorlands to dramatic coastal paths.

🤝

Community

No one hikes alone in Hyke It Up. We look after each other on the trail and celebrate together at the end.

💚

Wellbeing

Mental and physical health sit at the heart of everything we do. Fresh air, movement and connection are powerful medicine.

🌍

Respect for Nature

We leave no trace, follow the countryside code and actively support conservation of the landscapes we love.
